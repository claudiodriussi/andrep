**ESP32-WROOM-32** — dual-core Xtensa LX6 at 240 MHz, 4 MB flash, 520 KB SRAM.

Connectivity: **Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n** + **Bluetooth 4.2/BLE** on-module antenna.
Peripherals: 18x ADC, 2x DAC, 3x SPI, 2x I2C, 2x I2S, 3x UART, 1x CAN, hall sensor.

- Operating voltage: 3.0–3.6 V (5 V tolerant via on-board LDO)
- Deep sleep current: 10 µA — ideal for battery-powered IoT nodes
- Supported by ESP-IDF, Arduino core and MicroPython
- FCC/CE/IC certified for direct product integration
